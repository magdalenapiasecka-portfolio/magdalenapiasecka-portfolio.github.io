desktop and mobile app project

as a part of recruitment assignment, I was asked to design a hi-fi desktop and app mockups in the spirit of a key visual.
provided by the company. the task was to design a user panel that included:

  • user's name
  • menu, that includes following sections: auctions, followed acutions, put up for sale, information, privacy policy, your auctions (active element)
  • table for 'your auctions' section that includes columns: ID of the offer, name of the offer, date of uploading the offer, date of last activity, go to details
  • notification, a short message that informs that someone just placed an offer for one of the user's auctions
