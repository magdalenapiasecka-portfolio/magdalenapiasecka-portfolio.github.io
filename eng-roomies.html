overview

aims of the project

  1. identifyig and understading the needs and expectations of people who are looking for roommates and people who are looking for tenants
  2. creating and validating an app project
  3. ilustrating my design process

what is Roomies?

  • a space for people who are looking for an appartment
  • allows to know the preferences of potential candidates for roommates with only a few finger-taps
  • allows the compatibility between rommates that help build positive relationships and increases comfort of living
  • minimalizes the stress induced by meeting new people

challenges in the project

creating a solution for people who complain about their roommates and the numerosity of various platforms that offer spaces for rent

design process
(user centered design)

  1. researching users' needs
  2. designing an app draft version + information architecture
  3. usability tests
  4. implementating changes derived from usability tests and finalizing the UI
